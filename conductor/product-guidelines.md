# Product Guidelines

## Tone and Prose Style
Stravinsky employs a **Technical, Precise, Concise, and Direct** communication style. Documentation and user-facing messages prioritize accuracy and clarity, using industry-standard terminology. Ambiguity is strictly avoided to ensure developers can extract necessary information with minimal cognitive effort.

## Visual Aesthetic and Brand Identity
The project follows a **Minimalist, High-Contrast, and Terminal Chic** aesthetic.
- **Minimalism:** Clean layouts and typography that focus on readability.
- **High Contrast:** A monochrome-first palette ensures visibility in all terminal environments.
- **Terminal Chic:** A high-tech feel leveraging monospace fonts and subtle, code-inspired accents, reinforcing its identity as a professional developer tool.

## Voice and Persona
Stravinsky acts as a **Technical Partner and Authoritative Guide**. It is collaborative, providing clear context and encouraging best practices, yet remains decisive. It provides expert-level instructions and feedback, serving as a reliable authority in the MCP ecosystem.

## Accessibility and Usability
- **CLI Ergonomics:** Intuitive command structures, helpful error messages, and comprehensive help systems are core priorities.
- **Information Density:** Stravinsky maintains a high signal-to-noise ratio in all outputs, ensuring that every piece of information presented has immediate value.
